Young Leaders Collective

Adventure over the love of ease.''__Robbert Kennedy.

Young Leaders Collective(YLC) is a six months-long fellowship for exceptional individuals across sectors & aims to provide a platform to harness and augment the fellowsâ€™ skills while enabling Social Change. This fellowship will run concurrent to the existing vocations of the fellows. YLC will offer curated opportunities & formats for driven, young leaders to leverage their skills & network, beyond their work place, to engineer positive social change.

Why do we need the program

To make socail change more inclusive through wider participation from community members

To curate a tribe of youth leaders who are empahtic, creative and commited to bring socail cahnge

To provide an effective platform to youth leadres to engineer social cahnge by leverging their unique skills.

Who is iT For

Emerging leaders who are open for immersion into a reflective any socially impactfull program to find their true north.

Young profession who have a desire to contribute and leverage their skills & expertise towrads socail cahnge

Promising leadres who are keen to hone and further inhance their leadreship skills.

What will be learn

Empathy-Experience & engage with the new world with a new perspactive that allows you to understand & connect better with people around you

Creativity:An opportunities to design from scatch and execute a project that channelizes Support for impact initiatives serving vulernable communities.

Leadership leading a micro movements that call out for enrolling supports & resources beyond the realm of designations & work enviornment, will help you grow as a leaders

How does it work

Orchested engagement for six month concurrent to the existing vocation of fellows

local immersion to connect & help build a perspective on socail cahnge

Master classes with inspirational leadrers

Diverse, cross Sectional cohort

Ingenious Capstone project to faciliate positive social change

The Program

26 weeks long engagement. Requires commitment of 60 hours ( ~ mainly weekened and few weekdays) on behalf of fellow to beneift from & add value to the program.

phase1 introduction and immersion -virtual, and on site visits, engagement with akshaypatra leadership

Phase2:learning Session through master classes with industrty leaders

phase3:-Capstone Project -Work on project that will mobilizie Support impact intiatives

phase4:- Reflection -Review the journey from immersion to action
